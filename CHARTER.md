# The Weight of Nations Project — Charter

*One page. The moral center of the project: it seeds the README, the website intro, and the
skill's core promise. If a decision ever conflicts with this charter, the charter wins.*

The **Weight of Nations Project** exists to maintain a public, reproducible, ruler-labeled
account of world GDP shares, population shares, and output multipliers across deep history and
the modern world.

- It **separates the rulers**: nominal current-dollar GDP, PPP / international-dollar GDP,
  population share, and the productivity **multiplier** (GDP share ÷ population share). It never
  collapses them into one number.
- It treats **datasets as instruments with vintages**, not as timeless truth — and keeps the
  source lanes (Maddison, World Bank WDI/QoG, IMF WEO, UN WPP, Bairoch) deliberately separate.
- It asks readers to **read the ruler first, the direction second, and the decimal point last.**
- It publishes **fixed, dated editions for readers** and **living, openly-licensed materials for
  researchers** — and is complete as a dated snapshot even if never updated again.
- It welcomes **reuse, correction, and extension**, while preserving source discipline and a
  human editorial gate on every official release.
- It is **analysis, not advice**: GDP share is not identical to power; technology, institutions,
  energy, demographics, and political capacity decide how output becomes power. The project keeps
  that humility.
